About Us

Vardaan Enterprises


Vardaan Enterprises with its brand Sukh Sangam is a registered manufacturing unit under Khadi and Village Industries Commission (KVIC) - A Department of Ministry of Micro Medium and Small Enterprises (MSME), Govt. Of India. We are in business last 5 years and now we are in manufacturing since 9 months. We purely manufacturer organic and herbal Incense sticks and Dhoop Sticks. Our Product catagory includes Herbal Incense sticks, Black Incense sticks, White Incense sticks, Herbal Dhoop, Coloured Dhoop. All these products are manufactured using organic and herbal raw materials, aromatic fragrances and essential oils.


Available in various fragrances, each aroma would give you a very calming & peaceful sensation, while making you feel extremely relaxed during your prayer time. We, Vardaan Enterprises, situated at Sadguru Dham Guruwadi, Dahanu, Maharashtra, provide the best and prompt qulaity dhoop and agarbatti products to our clients. The offered products are processed by utilizing top quality basic material & sophisticated technology.
